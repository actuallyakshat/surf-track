Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Surf Track. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Surf Track collects the following types of information:

  • Browsing Data: We track the amount of time you spend on different websites. This includes the URLs of the websites you visit.
  • Local Storage Data: All collected data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with insights into your browsing habits by tracking the time spent on various websites.
  • To help you manage your online activities by monitoring your screen time.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to third parties. All data is stored locally on your device, and you have full control over it.

Your Choices

You have control over the data collected by Surf Track. You can:

  • Clear your browsing data from within the extension's settings.
  • Uninstall the extension to stop any further data collection.

Security

We are committed to protecting your data. We implement security measures to ensure your data remains safe and secure.
